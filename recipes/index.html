Vegan Recipes (On a Budget)

Welcome to my collection of affordable, delicious vegan recipes! As a student, I understand the importance of eating well while keeping costs down. Here I share my favorite plant-based meals that are kind to both your wallet and the planet.

My Favorite Recipes

  • Khichdi for Tummy Aches - A soothing, healing rice and lentil dish
  • PB&K (Kimchi Peanut Butter Tofu Sandwich) - A unique fusion sandwich with bold flavors

Why Budget Vegan Cooking?

  • Economical: Staples like beans, rice, and seasonal vegetables are among the most affordable foods
  • Sustainable: Plant-based eating reduces your environmental footprint
  • Healthy: Whole food vegan meals provide essential nutrients without breaking the bank

Pantry Essentials

Building a well-stocked pantry is the foundation of budget cooking. Here are my must-have items:

  1. Grains: Rice, oats, pasta, quinoa
  2. Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, black beans, split peas
  3. Seasonings: Nutritional yeast, curry powder, Italian herbs, soy sauce
  4. Canned goods: Tomatoes, coconut milk, vegetable broth
  5. Frozen vegetables: Peas, corn, spinach, mixed vegetables

Shopping Tips

  • Buy in bulk when possible
  • Shop seasonal produce
  • Check international grocery stores for better prices on spices and staples
  • Plan meals around weekly sales
  • Embrace "imperfect" produce

I'll be updating this page regularly with new recipes and tips. Have a budget-friendly vegan recipe to share? Email me with your suggestions!
