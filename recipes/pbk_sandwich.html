PB&K (Kimchi Peanut Butter Tofu Sandwich)

A unique fusion sandwich that combines the umami of kimchi with the richness of peanut butter and the protein of tofu.

About This Recipe

This might sound like an unusual combination, but the spicy, tangy kimchi pairs surprisingly well with creamy peanut butter, while the tofu adds protein and substance. It's a quick, satisfying meal that costs very little to make but delivers big on flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (for two people)
  • Half a block of tofu
  • Crunchy peanut butter
  • Kimchi
  • Fresh spinach
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 tbsp stir fry sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking (avocado oil recommended)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add tofu cubes to the pan
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of stir fry sauce and 1 tablespoon of honey
  4. Mix by gently moving the pan (don't use a spoon as it will make the tofu crumble)
  5. Once cooked, transfer tofu to a bowl
  6. Toast bread in a pan (no toaster allowed)
  7. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  8. Spread mashed avocado on another slice of bread
  9. Place kimchi on top of the avocado
  10. Add spinach on top of the peanut butter
  11. Add the tofu in between and close the sandwich
  12. Enjoy your trip to heaven!

Variations

  • Extra Crunch: Add thinly sliced cucumber or lettuce
  • Spicy Version: Add sriracha or hot sauce to the tofu glaze
  • Wrap Version: Use all ingredients in a tortilla wrap instead of bread

Budget Tips

Make your own kimchi when cabbage is in season to save money. Tofu is often cheaper at Asian grocery stores. This sandwich uses very little tofu per serving, making one block stretch across many meals.
